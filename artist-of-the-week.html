Artist of the Week

Raxx

Toronto Hip-Hop • Rap Artist • Canada

Raxx is a Toronto-based rap artist and songwriter with roots in Collingwood, known for confident lyricism, strong storytelling, and a dynamic flow that cuts through any beat. Influenced by both hip-hop and R&B, she blends sharp bars with a smooth, controlled delivery that feels polished while still remaining raw and authentic.

Her musical inspirations span generations — with Drake shaping her modern rap perspective and Amy Winehouse influencing her emotional depth and honesty in songwriting. This balance of confidence and vulnerability comes through clearly in both her recordings and live sets.

Watch how she performs “Mind Your Business”, a track that captures her energy, attitude, and undeniable presence. Off stage, Raxx keeps things grounded and relatable — currently obsessed with watching Love Island — while continuing to build momentum through performances, visuals, and fan engagement across Spotify, YouTube, and Instagram.
