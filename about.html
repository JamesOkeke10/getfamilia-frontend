About Get Familia

Get Familia is a performance platform focused on live music, honest talent, and high-quality visuals. We create sessions that feel intimate and real, while helping artists reach new audiences on YouTube and social media.

Whether you are performing in a small room or on a big stage, our goal is to capture that moment properly – clean audio, strong visuals and a story around the artist. We highlight upcoming voices in Afrobeat, R&B, Hip-Hop, Gospel and more.

What Get Familia Stands For
  • Showcase real, raw talent in a professional way.
  • Create a bridge between artists and new fans.
  • Provide content artists can proudly share and promote.
  • Build a supportive, family-style community around music.

Every performance, interview and feature is curated to feel premium yet authentic – no fake vibes.

How Get Familia Works

1
Discover & Select Artists

We scout upcoming talent through submissions, recommendations and live events, then select artists whose sound and story fit the Get Familia experience.

2
Plan the Session

Together with the artist, we plan the set list, location and visual style. The goal is simple: capture a performance that represents them correctly.

3
Publish & Promote

The final performance is released on our YouTube channel and shared across social platforms, with assets the artist can reuse for their own pages.

For Artists

  • Live or studio-style sessions filmed in high quality.
  • Potential “Artist of the Week” feature on the website.
  • Content you can share with fans, blogs and promoters.
